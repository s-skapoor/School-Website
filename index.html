School Classroom example

Was made in 2018-19 when the school was being built.

This place is a classroom where kids study and get taught by their teachers. There are a lot of classrooms in this school. This classroom is empty, but during school it contains a lot of chairs. It contains cubbies, a desk for the teacher, and a screen that can maximize whatever you put under it's camera. This is one of the best places to be in the school.

What's New

  • Virtual Learning

    During the pandemic, the Ella Baker Elementary decided to do school online, on Microsoft Teams. They decided to make channels for each subject, do meetings for those subjects, and give a chat box for people to talk and send messages. They even made a virtual camp during the 2020-21 school year. This was a very useful canteen.

  • Gaga Ball

    In the 2018-19 school year, the gaga ball pit was built on the school playground. It has become a very fun activity to do during recess. Today, it is played in the school everyday. This canteen was a very good idea.
